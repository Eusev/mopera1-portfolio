Project Screenshots

Membership Application Form - Upon an account's first login, users will be greeted with a welcome page where they can fill out and submit their membership application form.

Members’ Information Page - This is where each member can view their personal information as entered in their membership application form, along with their membership status, shared capital, and savings amount.

Members’ Loaning Page - The loan application page for users with approved membership. Members could select the type of loan, input their desired loan amount and term, and specify the purpose of their application. After clicking “Apply”, a message informs members that their loan application has been submitted and is awaiting for admins’ review and approval.

Members’ Loan Amortization Calculator Page - This page illustrates the loan amortization calculator page, where users calculated their future payments in a detailed format. Additionally, users had the option to print the schedule if desired.

Admin Pending Member Loans - This section allowed coop admins to view the list of members’ loan requests. Coop admins had the option to add the next payment date for loan transactions, approve loans, and reject loans, while they provided a reason for rejections.

Admin Geo-Location Page - Coop admins had access to this page, where they viewed the geolocations of their office branches. A filter option was available for the coop admins to select a specific office branch to see detailed information, including the total number of registered members, active and inactive members, and the general location of members marked on the map. Member locations were based on the barangay or city provided in their membership application forms. It also showed the section where company assignments could be viewed. Coop admins saw the number of members and announcements in the system for each company assignment.

Admin Announcement Page - The admin announcements list had a similar appearance to the members’ announcement page. However, on the coop admin side, there were additional capabilities to update or delete the announcements.
